Procedure For Admission
  1. Admission Procedure:COER offers admission to all eligible applicants. The admissions are done through counseling and seat allotment procedure of all the GATE qualified applicants. The applicants who have not qualified GATE will have to appear for the College entrance Test/Counselling. First, preference will be given to candidates who have qualified the GATE exam.
